Common questions

• How do I cancel my Pro subscription? Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → EasyEats → Cancel. Your Pro features stay active until the end of the current billing period.
• My HealthKit steps aren't showing. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → EasyEats and enable Steps and Active Energy. Then open EasyEats — the dashboard refreshes on launch.
• AI nutrition estimates look wrong. AI estimates are best-guess and can be edited. Tap any macro number on the AI result to correct it before saving.
• Restoring purchases on a new device. On the Profile tab, tap "Restore Purchases". You must be signed into the same Apple ID that bought the subscription.
• Deleting data. Swipe to delete any food entry from the Dashboard, or delete the entire app from your home screen to wipe all locally stored data.

Report a bug or request a feature

EasyEats Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 31, 2026

EasyEats ("we", "us", "the app") is a nutrition-tracking app developed by Julius Matanguihan. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

What we collect

• Food logs and goals you enter. Names, calories, macros, meal types, dates, daily targets. Stored locally on your device using Apple SwiftData. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
• HealthKit data (optional). If you grant access, we read step count and active energy from Apple Health to display on your dashboard. This data stays on-device and is never sent off your device by us.
• Photos and text you submit for AI nutrition analysis. When you use AI photo recognition or AI nutrition estimation, the relevant image or food name is sent to Anthropic (Claude API) for processing and a result is returned. We do not store these submissions on our own servers; Anthropic processes them under their own privacy policy (https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy).
• Subscription status. If you purchase EasyEats Pro, RevenueCat receives your anonymous Apple subscription receipt to verify entitlement. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy. We do not receive your Apple ID, name, email, or payment details — those stay with Apple.
• Camera and photo library access. Used only to capture or pick food photos at your request. We do not access these without your explicit action.

What we do not collect

• We do not have user accounts. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers for advertising, or any analytics.
• We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties for marketing.
• We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or trackers.

Children

EasyEats is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

• You can delete any logged food at any time from the Dashboard.
• You can revoke HealthKit access in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → EasyEats.
• You can revoke Camera or Photos access in Settings → EasyEats.
• Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
